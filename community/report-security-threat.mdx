## Responsible Disclosure

We take **security very seriously** at AI Interview Buddy.

If you discover a vulnerability, data leak, or any behavior that may compromise the privacy or security of our users or infrastructure:

**Please report it immediately by emailing:**  
[infra@aiinterviewbuddy.com](mailto:infra@aiinterviewbuddy.com)

> Do not post security issues publicly.  
> We appreciate responsible disclosure and will respond as quickly as possible.
