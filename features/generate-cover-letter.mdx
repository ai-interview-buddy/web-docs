---
title: "Generate a Cover Letter"
description: "Use AI to generate tailored cover letters based on your CV and the job description."
---

Inside any job position, you can generate a **customized cover letter**.

The AI uses:
- Your uploaded CV
- The job description (from URL or pasted content)

The letter will:
- Address the role specifically
- Highlight your key strengths
- Reflect a professional and personalized tone

> ✍️ You can regenerate or manually edit the content before downloading or using it.
